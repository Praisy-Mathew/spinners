About Us

About us

Healthy Eating. Honest Making.

At SPINNERS, we believe shawarma is a culture, not just food. While today’s shawarmas often compromise on quality and hygiene, we’re here to bring back the authentic Arabic and Turkish tradition. Prepared in closed, sanitized kitchens with farm-fresh meat, real Arabic spices, and zero artificial additives, every SPINNERS shawarma delivers purity, flavour, and trust in every bite.

Every shawarma from SPINNERS is a promise of purity.

MISSION

Our mission at SPINNERS is to redefine what real shawarma should be. We aim to build a healthier food culture by serving shawarmas that are crafted with purity, honesty, and care. Every ingredient we use — from farm-fresh meat to natural Arabic spices — reflects our commitment to clean eating and true flavour. We strive to provide a food experience that customers can trust, where hygiene is uncompromised, taste is authentic, and wellbeing is always prioritized. Through consistency, quality, and transparency, we work every day to set a new benchmark for hygienic shawarma in our community.

VISION

Our vision is to make SPINNERS a symbol of trust, health, and authentic shawarma culture across the region. We aspire to revive and lead the true Arabic–Turkish shawarma tradition, restoring its original purity in a world of shortcuts and artificial flavours. We aim to become a brand people choose not just for taste, but for the confidence of knowing their food is safe, hygienic, and genuinely wholesome. By promoting responsible food practices, inspiring healthier choices, and maintaining excellence in everything we prepare, we envision SPINNERS becoming the most respected name in hygienic shawarma — a brand that stands for quality without compromise.
