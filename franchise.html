TYPE OF FRANCHISE

We introduced 3 types of shop models for the convenience of Franchisee to select accordingly

1. KIOSK MODEL

A small, compact quick-service retail space under 300 sq.ft, ideal for rush roads, railway stations, airports, and other high-footfall areas. Designed for low investment, minimal staffing and fast service operations.

Definition: A small, compact retail space, often with an outdoor sitting area.
Location: High-footfall areas like busy streets, train stations, airports, etc.
Size: Very small — suitable for 2–3 staff, under 300 sq.ft.
Purpose: Ideal for quick-service food businesses.
Total Cost: Approx ₹15,00,000.
Franchise Fee: ₹3,00,000 + GST.
Royalty: 3.5% of total sales.
Breakeven: Around ₹4,00,000/month (~ ₹12,000/day average).

Salary Structure:
Shawarma Master – Around ₹30,000/month
Shawarma Helper cum Cleaner – Around ₹24,000/month
Manager cum Customer Service – Around ₹24,000/month

Other Expenses:
Rent – Under ₹20,000/month
Electricity – Around ₹10,000/month

Menu: Shawarma items and soft drinks.

2. CAFÉ MODEL

A medium-size quick-service retail space under 700 sq.ft, ideal for busy commercial streets, transport hubs, railway stations, airports, and other high-footfall locations. Suitable for 4–6 staff with both indoor and outdoor seating options.

Definition: A medium-size retail space with indoor and outdoor seating.
Location: High-footfall areas like rush roads, train stations, airports, etc.
Size: Medium — suitable for 4–6 people, under 700 sq.ft.
Purpose: Ideal for quick-service restaurant businesses.
Total Cost: Approx ₹25,00,000.
Franchise Fee: ₹4,00,000 + GST.
Royalty: 2% of total sales up to ₹8,00,000, 3% from ₹8,00,001 to ₹11,00,000, 4% above ₹11,00,000.
Breakeven: Around ₹7,00,000/month (~ ₹21,000/day average).

Salary Structure:
Shawarma Master – Around ₹30,000/month
Shawarma Helper cum Cleaner – Around ₹24,000/month
Manager cum Customer Service – Around ₹24,000/month
Juicer cum Cleaner – Around ₹18,000/month
Cleaner – Around ₹18,000/month

Other Expenses:
Rent – Under ₹40,000/month
Electricity – Around ₹18,000/month

Menu: Shawarma items, juices, fried chicken, and optional Shawaya.

3. RESTROCAFÉ MODEL

A large-size premium retail space between 2000–3000 sq.ft offering indoor and outdoor seating, suitable for family dining, kids play zones, meetings, celebrations, and a relaxed café-style experience. Ideal for spacious ambience and high comfort service.

Definition: A large retail space with both indoor and outdoor seating areas.
Location: Peaceful areas with ample car parking, usually just outside the crowded city, offering a pleasant and welcoming ambience.
Size: Large — suitable for 10–14 staff, spread across 2000 to 3000 sq.ft.
Purpose: Ideal for kids play areas, outdoor meetings, couples, families, and premium dining experiences.
Total Cost: Approx ₹50,00,000.
Franchise Fee: ₹5,00,000 + GST.
Royalty: 2% + GST of total sales up to ₹12,00,000, 3% + GST above ₹12,00,000.
Breakeven: Around ₹12,00,000/month (~ ₹37,000/day average).

Salary Structure:
Shawarma Master – Around ₹30,000/month
Shawarma Helper cum Cleaner – Around ₹24,000/month
Manager cum Customer Service – Around ₹24,000/month
Juicer cum Cleaner – Around ₹18,000/month
Cleaner – Around ₹18,000/month
Al Faham Master – Around ₹27,000/month
Customer Area Cleaning – Around ₹18,000/month
Customer Service – Around ₹18,000/month

Other Expenses:
Rent – Under ₹50,000/month
Electricity – Around ₹28,000/month

Menu: Shawarma items, juices, fried items, desserts, Shawaya, Al Faham, rice varieties, falooda, kunafa, waffles, and more premium café-style dishes.

Franchisor Obligations

What We Provide

Training

The Franchisor will provide initial training for the Franchisee and key employees on operational standards, recipes, and hygiene protocols.

Support

Continuous support will be provided in the form of operational manuals, regular audits, and assistance with marketing strategies.

Operation Standards

The Franchisor will provide the staff, menu as well as other ingredients for running the shop smoothly within the agreement period.

Social Media Advertisement

The social media pages for the Spinners café will be handled by the franchisor itself and will have only a single page (not allowed to form separate pages for each shop).
Social media maintaining fee: 5000 for Kiosk, 6000 for Café, and 7000 for Restro Café.

Franchisee Obligations

Franchisee Obligations

Location Confirmation

Location Confirmation: Shop location, water connection, three-phase connectivity, waste management, etc., should be arranged by the franchisee in coordination with the franchisor.

License and Documents

License and Documents: The shop license, food safety license, and other shop documents should be carried out by the franchisee.

Preparation Standards

Preparation standards: The colour code of the shop, tiles, ACP and the other interior elements such as table, chair, signboards, Camera, Music, kitchen floor tap etc., must be approved by the franchisor before purchase or installation.

Operational Standards

Operational Standards: The Franchisee agrees to maintain hygiene, quality, and service standards as specified by the Franchisor. Any change in serving or preparing the food item should be approved by the franchisor.
